Hello, I am Zach Lemis, an aspiring UX/UI Designer based in Denver, Colorado

My passion is focused on blending design with health and fitness. I recently moved to Denver where I hope to marry my two passions into a new career. I studied Architecture and Design at the University of Michigan, where I also started my coaching career as a Strength and Conditioning Coach for a university club team. Over the last 8 years, I spent the majority of my time managing fitness facilities, running operations and marketing strategy. I currently run two personal businesses: Zach Lemis Designs and Wolverine Weightlifting.
